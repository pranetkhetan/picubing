2x2x2

The 2x2x2 rubiks cube is the easiest one to solve, and thus requires only 3 major algorithms. Only the algorithms are listed here, for complete tutorials on how to use them please visit our Youtube channel, PI Cubing. If you don't have a 2x2x2 cube at home, you can use the above simulator to practice. Pressing the small reverse button on the bottom left corner return the simulated cube to its original state. It is recommended to open this simulator on a laptop for large size and easy controls.

This is the first algorithm required to solve the first layer after solving the white side and keeping the solved part at the back is :

R' D R L D' L R' D R

For a complete tutorial and instructions, please click on the video link given at the beginning of this page. The next algorithm required to solve the yellow face without spoiling the first layer is:

R U R' U R U2 R'

More specifications are given in the video. The last algorithm to solve the cube is:

R' F R' B2 R F' R B2 R2 B2

With these major algorithms, and our amazing tutorial videos, we hope you have enjoyed solving the 2x2x2 cube!
