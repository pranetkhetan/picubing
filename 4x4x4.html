4x4x4

The process of solving the 4x4x4 is mostly intuitive, and there are only 2 parity algorithms you need to remeber. Only the algorithms are listed here, for complete tutorials on how to use them please visit our Youtube channel, PI Cubing. If you don't have a 4x4x4 cube at home, you can use the above simulator to practice. Pressing the small reverse button on the bottom left corner return the simulated cube to its original state. It is recommended to open this simulator on a laptop for large size and easy controls.

The algorithm for the 4x4x4 OLL/Flipped edge parity is below:

r2 B2 U2 l U2 r' U2 r U2 F2 r F2 l' B2 r2

The algorithm for the PLL/Corner parity is below:

(Uu)2 (Ll)2 U2 l2 U2 (Ll)2 (Uu)2 F' U' F U F R' F2 U F U F' U' F R

With these major algorithms, and our amazing tutorial videos, we hope you have enjoyed solving the 4x4x4 cube!
