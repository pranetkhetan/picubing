Pyraminx

It is said that the pyraminx is next in easiness after the 2x2x2. It also required very few algorithms. Only the algorithms are listed here, for complete tutorials on how to use them please visit our Youtube channel, PI Cubing.

Note that the notation of a pyraminx is different from other cubic puzzles. Details are given in the video. The only algorithms needed to memorise are the ones for the last layer of the pyraminx. The algorithm for the 'Eyes' case of the last layer of the pyraminx is:

R' L R L' U L' U' L

The algorithm for the 'Symmetrical' case of the last layer of the pyraminx is:

R' U' R U' R' U' R

The algorithm for the 'Right Eye' case of the last layer of the pyraminx is:

R' L R L' U' L' U L

The algorithm for the 'Left Eye' case of the last layer of the pyraminx is:

L R' L' R U R U' R'

With these major algorithms, and our amazing tutorial videos, we hope you have enjoyed solving the pyraminx!
