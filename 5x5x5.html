5x5x5

The process of solving the 5x5x5 is mostly intuitive, and there are only a few normal algorithm and a few parity algorithms you need to remember. Only the algorithms are listed here, for complete tutorials on how to use them please visit our Youtube channel, PI Cubing. If you don't have a 5x5x5 cube at home, you can use the above simulator to practice. Pressing the small reverse button on the bottom left corner return the simulated cube to its original state. It is recommended to open this simulator on a laptop for large size and easy controls.

The algorithm for the last corner pieice in the 5x5x5 centers is:

l' U r U' l U r'
